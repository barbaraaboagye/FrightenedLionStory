The Lion's Story

One night, Lion is getting ready for bed. He reads his favorite bedtime story and sings his favorite bedtime song. He turns on his night light. Then he hears a sound. Tap, tap. "What's that sound?" he whispers. He grabs a baseball bat and a bicycle helmet. He hides under the bed. Suddenly, a little frog jumps on the windowsill. The frog is wearing a pink tutu and tap shoes. She wanted to show him new dance moves! Lion feels ridiculous.
